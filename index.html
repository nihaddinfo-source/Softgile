03 · Our Solutions

Solutions designed for controlled, visible, scalable service operations

The solution model below combines Softgile’s service portfolio with the ASOA framework and Atlassian-powered ITSM capabilities.

ASOA foundation

Adaptive Service Operations Architecture frames ITSM as an event-driven, interconnected service platform with detection, control, intelligence, execution, and data layers. In practice, this means faster routing, richer context, safer changes, and stronger traceability.

Detection Control Intelligence Execution Data
⇄

Change, Incident, Problem, Access & Service Request Management

A flagship service layer for responsive support, controlled change, and streamlined access and request fulfillment.

  • Incident triage, enrichment, routing, and SLA-driven response
  • Problem management with root-cause capture and knowledge reuse
  • Change workflows with approvals, risk checkpoints, and rollback awareness
  • Service request templates and catalog-driven fulfillment
  • Access lifecycle support aligned to joiner, mover, leaver scenarios
Best for: organizations needing structured, scalable service delivery across IT and business teams.
⌘

IT Asset Management

Bring hardware, software, licenses, and asset relationships into one visible, governed operating model.

  • Centralized asset inventory and lifecycle visibility
  • Tracking for equipment, software, licenses, and vendors
  • Integration with Jira and Atlassian tools
  • Compliance support and optimized IT spending
Best for: teams that need stronger control over assets, costs, and configuration visibility.
▣

PMO

Support predictable delivery through planning, tracking, reporting, and cross-team coordination built around Jira-driven execution.

  • Backlog visibility and work organization
  • Dashboards and reporting for transparency
  • Delivery management involvement where needed
  • Workflow structure that scales with project complexity
Best for: PMOs and delivery leaders seeking clearer oversight, accountability, and execution flow.
🛡

Risk / Audit Management

Strengthen operational governance with approvals, traceability, audit support, security controls, and measurable performance.

  • CAB and ECAB-style approvals for controlled change
  • Audit-ready records, reports, and presentation support
  • Security and access control alignment with Atlassian Guard
  • Traceability across incidents, changes, services, and decisions
Best for: regulated, high-risk, or control-oriented environments requiring stronger governance.
